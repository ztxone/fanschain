How it works?

What is

FansChain

Blockchain platform that creates a new way to invest and monetize content in the music market.


Artists will get a new kind of content monetization, and fans will get the opportunity to invest directly in musicians and earn money from the growth of their popularity.

Social currency

1

The artist releases his own token on the platform (releases / publishes) - Similar to stock issue

2

The token gives access to the Artist`s exclusive content with no other way to get it.

3

The more promising the content and more popular the Performer is, the more expensive his token is and more users want to buy it - the reverse situation is also possible.

4

Tokens sale money is transferred to the artist or label, and fans will trade tokens and earn on the price increase.
